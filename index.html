Interesting Facts About Doves

  1. The word pigeon is often used to call the larger species, while the dove is for calling the smaller species in ornithology
  2. The most common bird in the world is dove. It can be found in Asia, America, Australia or even Africa
  3. The nest of dove is often spotted on the ground, ledges or even trees. They build the nest from the sticks




Interesting Facts About iguanas

  1. Iguanas during the times of attack; use their tails to punch the enemy. Also, they can detach a part of their tail in danger and ensure a fast escape
  2. On the top of their heads; these lizards have a third eye, which is a retina-like structure that connects to the pineal gland in the brain
  3. Iguanas who can be very stable and safe in the trees can occasionally fall but what is interesting is that they can survive great falls even from the height of 40-50 feet and that too without any injuries
